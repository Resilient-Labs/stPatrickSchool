About St Patrick School

Mission Statement

Saint Patrick School a Roman catholic school has been educating the students of the greater Boston since 1887.

Saint Patrick School is commited to serving generations of students and families. Providing Catholic doctrine, Christian values and a strong academic curriculum in a nurturing and peaceful environment.

Saint Patrick School continues to teach values of truth, morality and spirituality to a richly diverse population.

Philosophy

As a Catholic school, Saint Patrick School's priority is to provide a quality education that includes the development of the whole person: spiritually, intellectually, socially, emocionally and psychologically. We believe that coorporation is the hallmark of Saint Patrick School and the school community is encouraged to contribute to the cooperative effort of education, fund-raising and volunteering. The standard life in the school community includes: Christian moral virtues of loving concern and respect for others, mutual trust, non-violence, service to those in need and justice to all.

Our Catholic Identity

St. Patrick School is a Catholic school and the beliefs and moral teachings of the Catholic Church are reflected in all studies. St. Patrick School admits children of all faiths. Students are expected to participate in daily prayer, religion classes, as well as school liturgies, and prayer services throughout the school year.
